About Us

The Rotaract Club of Women's Christian College is a dynamic community of student leaders committed to service, leadership, fellowship and personal growth. Guided by Rotary values, the club strives to create meaningful and sustainable impact both within the college campus and in the wider community.

Our Legacy

Chartered on 10 May 1995, the Rotaract Club of Women's Christian College has proudly nurtured generations of young changemakers. Through social service initiatives, leadership development programs, community outreach projects and collaborative partnerships, the club continues to uphold a legacy of excellence and service above self.
